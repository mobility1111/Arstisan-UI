How It Works

Simple steps to connect artisans with customers

01
Register Artisan

Artisans register and submit their skills, experience, and location to join our network.

02
Request Service

Customers request for an artisan through our user-friendly app or website platform.

03
Smart Matching

Our system intelligently assigns the nearest available artisan to the customer request.

04
Job Done & Payment

Artisan completes the job, receives payment, and you earn your commission seamlessly.
